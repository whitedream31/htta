Welcome

Our friendly association aims to promote friendship and understanding between different European countries.

Our Twin Towning Association

Find out more about our association.

Culture

Through exchange visits we learn about other cultures and a programme of entertainment provides trips to local places of interest. It can also be an opportunity to learn the basis of another language.

Social

We organise frequent social events for our members including themed meals, quiz nights and other social activities.

Friendship

We have forged many friendships and introduced our children and grandchildren to other ways of life with our inexpensive visits.
